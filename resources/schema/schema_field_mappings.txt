============================================================
TWO-TOWER FIELD MAPPINGS
User <-> Plant schema alignment for matching features
============================================================

User constraints.preferred_size  <->  Plant Info.size
  Enum: small | medium | large

User environment.light_level      <->  Plant Care.light_req.ideal_light.sunlight_bucket
  (also consider tolerated_light.sunlight_bucket for match logic)
  Enum: direct | bright_light | bright_indirect | indirect | diffused

User environment.humidity_level  <->  Plant Care.humidity_req_bucket
  Enum: low | medium | high

User preferences.care_level      <->  Plant Care.care_level
  Enum: easy | medium | hard

User preferences.care_preferences.watering_freq  <->  Plant Care.water_req_bucket
  Enum: low | medium | high

User climate                     <->  Plant Care.climate
  Enum: Arid Tropical | Subtropical | Subtropical arid | Tropical | Tropical humid

User environment.temperature_pref (min_f, max_f)  <->  Plant Care.temp_req (min_temp, max_temp)
  Overlap logic: plant range must intersect user range (Fahrenheit)

User constraints.hard_no: frequent_watering  <->  Plant Care.water_req_bucket: high
  Filter: exclude plants with water_req_bucket=high when user has frequent_watering in hard_no
